# HAULR Design Specification

**Theme & Creative Direction:**
- Visual identity follows spotter.ai
- Black canvas, crisp white typography
- Spotter green accent (`#22C55E` provisional, verify AA contrast)
- Cinematic night-driving energy

**Typography:**
- UI: `Inter` or `Geist`
- Numbers/Counters: `JetBrains Mono`

**Motifs:**
- Radar/telemetry scanline loaders
- Pulses for GPS fixes
- Dashed lane-marker dividers
- Odometer flip counters

**Accessibility:**
- WCAG AA contrast for the green accent
- `prefers-reduced-motion` fallbacks (static poster)
